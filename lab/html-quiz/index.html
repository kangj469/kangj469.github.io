My favorite restaurant around Parsons is Tsurutontan.
They serve a variety of huge warm udon bowls and side dishes. The ambience of the restaurant is cozy and warm, which brings me back on chilly days.

My favorite dish as Tsurutontan is curry udon.

If you get a chance to dine at Tsurutontan, definitely try this dish out! Its creamy sauce is a perfect combination of both savory and sweet spices which gets along well with the soft and chewy texture of the udon noodles.
